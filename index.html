A Short Intro.

I received my B.S. in Aerospace Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU, Rank 105th in US News 2022) in 2018. And now, I am pursuing my Ph.D. degree in SJTU under the supervision of Prof. Xingqun Zhan and Dr. Yawei Zhai. My research interests include

  • Navigation Integrity: integrity monitoring algorithms for GNSS, GNSS/INS, and multi-sensor integration; navigation system integrity perfomance evaluation

  • Multi-Sensor Integration, e.g., GNSS/INS tight integration and GNSS/INS/Vision integration

  • Multi-Agent Cooperative Navigation: fusion strategy design, dynamic scheduling of fusion architecture, and integrity monitoring

The title of my Ph.D. thesis will be "Ensuring Navigation Integrity of Multi-Sensor Integration for Future Autonomous Systems", which is expected to be finished in June 2023. After graduation, I plan to do a two- or three-year postdoctoral research in a U.S. or E.U. university. I would appreciate it if you can offer/recommend a postdoc position to me!
